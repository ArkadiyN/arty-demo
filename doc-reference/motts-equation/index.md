# Mott's Equation — Reference Articles

Topic: Variable-range hopping (VRH) conductivity model by N.F. Mott — describes
low-temperature electrical transport in strongly disordered systems via
$$\\sigma(T) = \\sigma_0 \\exp\\left[-\\left(\\frac{T_0}{T}\\right)^{1/4}\\right]$$

### Article 1 — Summary

Proposes a 4-parameter calibration function derived from the Mott VRH model that fits
NTC thermistor resistance–temperature data over three decades of temperature (0.04 K – 40 K).
Includes both the $R(T)$ fit and its inverse $T(R)$, with uncertainty analysis.
Developed in the context of ESA's Athena X-ray observatory detector readout chain.
